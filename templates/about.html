About US

Know more about us and our Team.

What we do

We are a product development consultancy that specializes in customized hardware for automation , computation, security,test equipments, and other work specific/personal needs.
We are resourceful in mechatronics, electrical engineering, software engineering, design research, and prototyping. To put it in simpler terms, we make products that look good, work better, address the needs of users, and make a substantial impact on the market, all the while meeting the demands and needs of our clients. We not only come up with innovative ideas and concepts, we make them work.

Our Philosophy

At Deshik Labs, you will find a group of avid problem solvers. Our individual experiences are broad and diverse. Our involvement with a wide variety of product developments allow us to apply what we learned to each new project. We call this compounding experience. We all share the hunger to never stop learning, which keeps us on the cutting edge of product development. We are creative and we are innovative. We are equally comfortable creating innovative ideas as we are turning them into something that works.
The name deshik means guru in Sanskrit,deshik is associated with innovation, someone who is very imaginative and creative,has a urge to build/create something new ,hence the name..
